Website Choice 
Name: Novamobile
Organisation Description: 
The name of the business is Novamobile. The business offers new and pre-owned phones and laptops for all sorts of customers at affordable prices for those who have strict budgets.

Reasons for Choosing the Organisation: 
I specifically chose this business because of my interest in tech and combating the unavailability of affordable mobile devices and laptops in South Africa.
I primarily chose the iPhone mobile device because it is the most desired by people in this country and acquiring one is not cheap. The same goes for Laptops which have become a huge necessity to students and people in general. That’s where Novamobile comes in to provide affordable mobile devices and laptops for everyone.
Website Goals and Objectives:
The website will help the business reach a wider market and because most people prefer to shop online rather than go to the actual store making it convenient for them. The website will also make it easy for the business to showcase its products and services by helping with marketing and promotions. A website also helps the businesses credibility with customer reviews online. The mission is to provide customers with affordable tech while delivering service and value.
