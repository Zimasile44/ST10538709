Return & Repair Information

If you are not satisfied with your purchase or your device has a fault, you may request a return or repair within 7 to 14 days of receiving your order, depending on the warranty policy.

Returns

The device must be unused, in original packaging, and accompanied by proof of purchase.

Repairs

Devices covered under warranty will be repaired free of charge for manufacturing faults. Customer damage may incur repair fees.

Process

Contact our support team with your order number and describe the issue. Our team will guide you on the next steps.

For assistance, email support@novamobile.co.za or call +27 11 456 7890.
